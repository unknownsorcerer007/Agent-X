How Login Handoff Works

1
AI Detects Login Page
When the AI agent navigates to a login/signup page (Instagram, Twitter, Gmail, etc.), it automatically detects the login form and pauses automation.
2
Control Handed to You
The AI pauses and you get control of the browser. A banner appears here and in the browser view showing that login is needed. Your credentials are NEVER seen by the AI.
3
You Log In Manually
If the browser is running in non-headless mode, type your credentials directly in the browser window. If headless, use the Debug Terminal to interact. Your password goes ONLY to the real website.
4
Click "I'm Done"
Once you've finished logging in, click the "I'm Done Logging In" button. The AI verifies new auth cookies, saves your session, and resumes automation with your logged-in state.
